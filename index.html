Wearable System for Monitoring Spinal Posture

About Our Project

We are developing an innovative wearable system designed to monitor and improve spinal posture in real-time. This technology aims to prevent back problems and promote better health through continuous posture monitoring and intelligent feedback. Our project combines advanced sensors, data analysis, and user-friendly design to create a practical solution for posture correction.
